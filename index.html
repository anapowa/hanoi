Tower of Hanoi

Instructions:

  1. Click on a section to pick up a disc.
  2. Click on a different section to drop the disc.
  3. Repeat until all the discs have been moved to the right-most section.

Rules:

  • Only one disc can be moved at a time.
  • Only upper disc from one of the stacks can be moved by placing it on top of another stack.
  • No disk can be placed on top of a smaller disk
